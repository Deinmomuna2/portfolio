USER JOURNEY AND STORY MAPPING:

After a brainstorming session with my client and team, we came up with our project goals in line with the target users. I was able to come up with the user journey and story map taking note of the most critical and primary goals.


The image below is just a rough diagram:


SKETCHING:

I began to sketch out my ideas from simple sketches with just shapes and lines to more complex sketches with annotations and titles.



WIREFRAMES:

I began to draw my wireframes from my sketches but with more informations and elements. They were not perfect but they represented an oversight of what the product would look like.



TYPOGRAPHY AND COLOR THEORY:

I decided to go for colors that matches the brand as you see it displayed in the images below and out of so many great fonts, I finally chose 'Lora' for headings and 'Inter' for body and buttons, though at different weights and sizes for different elements.



USABILITY TEST:

I carried out usability test and got come constructive feedbacks.

  • USER 1:
    The colors seem to be a little too sharp. Try reducing the brightness so it doesn't alter ones view.
  • USER 2:
    The icons are too small. Try to make little adjustments so they could be easily clickable.


ITERATION:

With the constructive feedbacks gotten from users, I had to iterate on the design with my focus on concerned areas. This lead to my final design coming out better

  • USER 1:
    Yeah! this is great. The colors are awesome. Nice job.
  • USER 2:
    Great work. For me, this is perfect.
